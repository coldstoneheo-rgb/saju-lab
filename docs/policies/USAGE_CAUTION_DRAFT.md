# Usage Caution Draft

Status: Draft for paid-launch readiness. This is not a finalized legal policy.

Saju Lab provides report-first Saju interpretation for self-understanding, reflection, entertainment, and practical decision support. It does not provide deterministic predictions or professional advice.

## Core Scope

The product may describe:
- tendencies
- contexts
- recurring patterns
- practical reflection prompts
- career and finance themes
- uncertainty and missing-data impact

The product must not claim:
- guaranteed success
- exact money outcomes
- health events
- legal outcomes
- unavoidable failure
- fixed relationship outcomes
- professional investment, medical, legal, or psychological advice

## Paid Report Caution

Paid reports sell depth, structure, and convenience. Payment does not increase prediction certainty.

The paid detailed report must preserve:
- confidence level
- missing birth-time impact
- calculation transparency
- disclaimer language
- no-professional-advice boundary

## User Responsibility

Users should treat the report as one input for reflection. Important financial, medical, legal, career, or relationship decisions should be checked against real-world information and qualified professionals when needed.

## Copy Rules

Allowed language:
- "경향"
- "흐름"
- "가능성"
- "점검"
- "선택지"
- "참고 정보"

Avoid language:
- "반드시"
- "무조건"
- "보장"
- "확정"
- "정확한 예측"
- "전문 조언"
